How to Solve a Rubix Cube

A couple of things you need to know before solving a Rubix Cube for the first time:

  • Think inside the box
  • If you get frustrated, take a break!
  • You're not gonna learn it in one day.

There are six steps to solving a rubix cube

  1. solving the white cross
  2. solving the white corners
  3. solving the middle layer
  4. solving the yellow cross
  5. put the yellow corners in the right place
  6. orient yellow corners the right way
